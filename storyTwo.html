TRAPPED! An Unseen Letter

10th August 2022

My Dear Lily,

I know it is your birthday today. I can not make it this year either. I hope you know that daddy loves you very much — my baby girl. You turn 13. It seems just yesterday that I held you for the first time in my arms. 7 pounds and 19 inches. You were an angel. You still are.

The work here is great. The Shen-Li corporation is very generous. They take great care of me. Mr Li always is the heart of the building. He always cheers me up. Little Miss Li reminds me of the 5-year-old you. Always cheerful and indeed very playful. Even she is very interested in Xiener.

But I must warn you. I suspect that the corporation is up to no good. Mr Han keeps asking if Xiener can take over human brains. I don't like him very much.

But these things can wait. I have already booked tickets to take you to Disneyland! I know how much you wanted to go. Mr Li even arranged for VIP Passes. Mark the date in your calendar. We are going on the 22nd of August. And DON’T tell your mom about this. I want to keep it a surprise.

Love,

Dad.
